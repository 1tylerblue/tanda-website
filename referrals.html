Referral Rules

YOU ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR TRACKING YOUR REFERRALS

Each referrer must keep their own record of every submitted referral, including the customer name and submission date, so the referral can be identified and followed up.

  1. Referrers are responsible for keeping their own referral records and tracking the referrals they submit.
  2. Referral commission is 10% of the approved job value excluding GST.
  3. Commission is paid only after the referred customer has completed payment.
  4. Cancelled, refunded, unpaid, or disputed jobs do not qualify.
  5. Referrals must be submitted before the customer books, unless approved by T & A Pro Cleaning.
  6. Duplicate referrals are reviewed by T & A Pro Cleaning.
  7. Existing customers, existing leads, or jobs already quoted may not qualify unless approved.
  8. The referred customer must give permission to be contacted.
  9. T & A Pro Cleaning may reject referrals that are incomplete, inaccurate, unsafe, fraudulent, or outside service areas.
  10. Commission is not payable on GST, card surcharges, refunds, discounts, or third-party costs.
  11. For recurring subscription customers, default commission applies to the first approved paid service only unless T & A Pro Cleaning approves a different arrangement in writing.

This program is a referral arrangement only. It does not create employment, partnership, franchise, agency, or contractor status.
